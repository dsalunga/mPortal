      About ASOP

      Short for A Song of Praise Music Festival, ASOP is a songwriting event of the Members Church of God International (MCGI) dedicated to songs of praise to God.

      Leaders Mr. Synthetic153487 and Mr. Synthetic6b0fd6 launched it as a yearly festival in 2004 to produce more original compositions in the Church. In 2009 however, Mr. Synthetic0a5725 made ASOP bi-annual giving birth to the Mid-Year and Year-End Finals.

      After passing regional eliminations, finalists get to represent the Church division (based on geography) to which they belong. In the finals night, Mr. Synthetic0a5725 and Mr. Syntheticaa47f8 critique each song after being presented, and sometimes even give live lectures on quality songwriting. “God deserves the best,” they never fail to reiterate in ASOP events.

      The finals night is held at the Philippine headquarters of MCGI, the ADD Convention Center in Apalit, Pampanga. Finalists that cannot physically attend the event can have interpreters coming from the Philippines to sing their songs live on stage.

      Joining, and even watching ASOP, requires no fee. However, since the event’s inception, Mr. Synthetic0a5725 and Mr. Syntheticaa47f8 have given more than millions of pesos in prizes just to hear the best songs of praise that can be crafted by the hearts and minds of men.
